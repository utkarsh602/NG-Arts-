What We Do

Precision in Every Cut

CNC Works01
Computer-controlled precision cutting for complex geometries, custom signage, and industrial components. Micron-level accuracy on every pass — consistent across batches large or small.
Wood Carving02
Detailed 3D reliefs and intricate surface work on natural wood. Ideal for decorative panels, furniture embellishments, and architectural feature walls with organic character.
Grill Cutting03
Custom lattice and grill designs for windows, gates, and facades. Any pattern — geometric, floral, or bespoke — cut with clean edges and structural integrity intact.
2D Cuttings04
High-precision flat cutting in MDF, PVC, ACP, and PLY. Perfect for signage, interior panels, partitions, and exhibition displays requiring clean, burr-free edges.
